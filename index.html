Integration Steps

IoT + MATLAB Pipeline

1. Vision Source

Phone camera or webcam sends frames to the dashboard.

2. MATLAB Detection

MATLAB posts pest name, confidence, and marked zone into the local API.

3. ESP32 Telemetry

ESP32 pushes NPK, moisture, and temperature to the same backend.

4. Decision Layer

The dashboard combines pest and soil signals into one farm-health status.
