Road Map

Product Build Phases

Phase 01 Proof Of Detection

Connect the camera, draw the pest box, and verify MATLAB detections in the dashboard.

Phase 02 Field Node Pilot

Deploy the ESP32, local display, and moisture or climate sensing in real crop conditions.

Phase 03 Pilot Validation

Collect false positives, stability issues, and service pain points during trial runs.

Phase 04 Product Hardening

Move to enclosure, wiring, better sensors, and long-duration operation with real users.

System Flow

Roadmap Pipeline

1. Vision Source

Phone camera or webcam captures crop imagery from the field.

2. MATLAB Detection

MATLAB returns pest name, confidence, and bounding box into the API.

3. ESP32 Telemetry

The field node contributes moisture, humidity, and temperature readings.

4. Farm Decision Layer

The dashboard turns all of it into one readable action view for the farmer.
